1. Strategic Foundation

1.1 The product in one sentence

PhysioByRutvi helps people receive appropriate physiotherapy at home by matching them with a qualified physiotherapist and supporting that care through clear clinical standards led by Dr Rutvi Gandhi.

1.2 Recommended positioning

The right physiotherapist, at home—with care standards led by Dr Rutvi.

PhysioByRutvi should not behave like a generic directory and should not promise that Dr Rutvi personally handles every appointment. The brand advantage is a more considered middle ground:

More personal and accountable than an anonymous marketplace.

More flexible and scalable than a solo physiotherapist.

More human and condition-aware than a large home-healthcare call centre.

Clear about who will visit, how matching works and what the patient can expect.

1.3 Core promise

Patients receive:

A physiotherapist selected for the concern, location and availability.

A structured first assessment before a longer plan is recommended.

Continuity with the assigned physiotherapist wherever possible.

Clear explanations, home exercises and progress checkpoints.

Escalation or referral when home physiotherapy is not the appropriate next step.

1.4 Role of Dr Rutvi

Use this language consistently:

PhysioByRutvi was founded by Dr Rutvi Gandhi, PT, MPT, to make home physiotherapy easier to begin and more consistent to continue. She leads the care standards, therapist network and escalation process behind the service.

Do not claim that Dr Rutvi personally assesses, treats or replies to every patient unless that becomes an operational guarantee.

1.5 Brand voice

The voice should be:

Calm, not dramatic.

Clinically grounded, not overly medical.

Specific, not inspirational for its own sake.

Reassuring, not promising outcomes.

Human, without becoming casual about health.

Words to favour

Assess, understand, match, guide, progress, appropriate, qualified, practical, home visit, recovery goal, movement, function, next step.

Words to reduce or avoid

Miracle, cure, guaranteed, pain-free, fastest recovery, world-class, best, perfect, root cause, permanent relief, one specialist every visit.

1.6 Content ratio

60% useful service information.

25% credibility and proof.

15% emotional reassurance.

6. Condition Page — Back and Neck Pain

SEO

Title: Back and Neck Pain Physiotherapy at Home in Mumbai

Meta description: Home physiotherapy assessment for back pain, neck stiffness and movement-related discomfort across Bhayander to Andheri.

Hero

Eyebrow: BACK AND NECK PAIN

When sitting, sleeping or ordinary movement starts to hurt.

Back and neck symptoms can affect work, rest, lifting, driving and exercise. A home assessment looks beyond the painful area to understand movement, strength, daily habits and warning signs that may need medical review.

CTA: Check Home-Visit Availability

This service may be relevant if

Pain or stiffness increases with sitting, standing, bending or lifting.

Neck discomfort is linked with desk work or prolonged screen use.

Symptoms return repeatedly after temporary relief.

You are struggling to resume normal activity after a minor strain.

Your doctor has recommended physiotherapy.

What the physiotherapist may assess

Symptom history and aggravating movements.

Neck, back, hip and shoulder mobility where relevant.

Strength, control and tolerance for daily tasks.

Workstation, sleeping or lifting habits.

Sensation, reflexes or neural signs when indicated.

How home physiotherapy may help

Treatment may include education, movement modification, mobility work, hands-on techniques and progressive strengthening. The aim is not simply to provide temporary relief, but to make everyday movement more manageable and build the capacity required for work, exercise and daily life.

Seek medical advice promptly if

There is significant trauma, fever, unexplained weight loss, new weakness, worsening numbness, loss of bladder or bowel control, severe unrelenting pain or other symptoms that concern you.

FAQs

Should I take complete bed rest?

Complete rest is not routinely helpful for many common back and neck problems. The appropriate level of activity depends on symptoms and assessment.

Will treatment include massage?

Hands-on treatment may be used when appropriate, but it is only one part of care. Education, movement and progressive exercise are often important for longer-term improvement.

Can the physiotherapist assess my workstation?

Yes, when work setup appears relevant, the physiotherapist can review practical changes within the actual home or work-from-home environment.

7. Condition Page — Sports Injuries

SEO

Title: Sports Injury Physiotherapy at Home in Mumbai

Meta description: Assessment and progressive rehabilitation for sprains, strains and return to running, gym or sport through home physiotherapy in Mumbai.

Hero

Eyebrow: SPORTS INJURY REHABILITATION

Return to activity with a plan—not guesswork.

A sports injury needs more than waiting for pain to settle. Rehabilitation should rebuild the movement, strength, control and confidence required by the activity you want to return to.

CTA: Discuss Your Injury

This service may be relevant if

You have a recent sprain, strain or overuse injury.

Pain returns whenever training intensity increases.

You can manage daily activity but do not feel ready for sport.

You are returning after a period of rest, immobilisation or surgery.

You want a structured strengthening and return-to-activity plan.

What the physiotherapist may assess

Injury history, swelling, tenderness and movement tolerance.

Strength and control compared with the uninjured side.

Running, landing, jumping, lifting or sport-specific movement where appropriate.

Training load, recovery pattern and previous injuries.

Signs that require imaging or an orthopaedic opinion.

How rehabilitation may progress

Settle and protect

Reduce aggravation while maintaining safe movement and capacity.

Rebuild

Progress mobility, strength, balance and control based on the injured tissue and activity.

Reintroduce demand

Gradually restore running, lifting, change of direction or other activity-specific tasks.

Test readiness

Use functional checkpoints instead of relying only on the absence of pain.

FAQs

How soon after an injury should physiotherapy begin?

It depends on the injury. Some conditions benefit from early assessment and guided movement, while suspected fractures, dislocations or major tears need medical evaluation first.

Do I need gym equipment at home?

Not always. Many early exercises use body weight, bands or simple equipment. Later-stage rehabilitation may require access to heavier resistance or a suitable facility.

Can home physiotherapy prepare me fully for sport?

Home care can cover substantial parts of rehabilitation. High-speed, heavy-load or field-based testing may eventually require an appropriate gym, ground or sports setting.

10. Condition Page — Sciatica and Nerve-Related Leg Pain

SEO

Title: Sciatica Physiotherapy at Home in Mumbai

Meta description: Home assessment for lower-back-related leg pain, tingling and numbness, with guidance on movement, strength and when medical review is required.

Hero

Eyebrow: SCIATICA AND LEG SYMPTOMS

When pain or tingling travels into the leg.

Symptoms described as sciatica may come from different structures and can vary greatly in severity. Assessment helps identify how movement affects the symptoms and whether physiotherapy is an appropriate option.

CTA: Discuss Your Symptoms

Symptoms people commonly report

Pain travelling from the back or buttock into the thigh, calf or foot.

Tingling, pins and needles or altered sensation.

Symptoms aggravated by sitting, bending, coughing or walking.

Recurrent episodes linked with back pain.

What the physiotherapist may assess

Distribution and behaviour of symptoms.

Back, hip and lower-limb movement.

Strength, sensation and neural sensitivity.

Sitting, sleeping and activity tolerance.

Red flags requiring urgent medical assessment.

Important safety information

Seek urgent medical attention for new bladder or bowel changes, numbness around the groin or saddle area, rapidly worsening weakness, severe symptoms in both legs or other sudden neurological changes.

How physiotherapy may help

When appropriate, care may include education, positions or movements that reduce symptoms, gradual restoration of activity, neural mobility and progressive strengthening. Advice should be individual; generic stretching can aggravate some presentations.

FAQs

Should I stretch the painful leg?

Not automatically. Some nerve-related symptoms worsen with aggressive stretching. Assessment should guide the appropriate movement.

Do I need an MRI before physiotherapy?

Not everyone needs imaging before beginning conservative care. Medical review or imaging may be appropriate when symptoms, neurological findings or clinical history indicate it.

Can I continue working?

This depends on symptoms and job demands. The physiotherapist may suggest changes to sitting, movement breaks and activity while recovery progresses.

11. Condition Page — Frozen Shoulder

SEO

Title: Frozen Shoulder Physiotherapy at Home in Mumbai

Meta description: Home physiotherapy for painful shoulder stiffness affecting reaching, dressing and sleep, with stage-appropriate mobility and strengthening.

Hero

Eyebrow: FROZEN SHOULDER

Restore useful shoulder movement without forcing the process.

Frozen shoulder often develops gradually and can affect dressing, reaching overhead, fastening clothing and sleeping. Treatment should reflect the stage, irritability and movement available—not push through severe pain indiscriminately.

CTA: Discuss Shoulder Stiffness

What the physiotherapist may assess

Onset, pain pattern and night symptoms.

Active and assisted shoulder movement.

Neck and shoulder contribution.

Functional tasks such as dressing and reaching.

Medical factors or injuries that may require further review.

How physiotherapy may help

Depending on the stage, care may include pain-management education, gentle mobility, hands-on techniques, home exercises and later strengthening. Progress is usually measured through useful movement and function, not only a single range measurement.

FAQs

Should frozen shoulder exercises be painful?

Exercises may feel like a controlled stretch, but repeatedly provoking severe or lasting pain can be counterproductive. Intensity should match the stage and irritability.

How long does recovery take?

Frozen shoulder can take time and varies between people. Assessment helps set realistic expectations and monitor whether the pattern is typical.

Can diabetes affect frozen shoulder?

Frozen shoulder is more common in people with diabetes and may behave differently. Relevant medical history should be shared with the physiotherapist.

CTA

Tell us which shoulder movements have become difficult.

Share when the stiffness began, whether sleep is affected and any relevant medical history.

13. Condition Page — Posture and Work-Related Discomfort

SEO

Title: Posture and Workstation Physiotherapy at Home in Mumbai

Meta description: Home assessment for neck, back and shoulder discomfort associated with prolonged sitting, repetitive work and workstation setup.

Hero

Eyebrow: POSTURE AND WORK-RELATED DISCOMFORT

Make your work setup support movement—not replace it.

There is no single perfect posture that prevents every symptom. The more useful goal is a setup that reduces unnecessary strain, allows position changes and supports the strength and movement your work demands.

CTA: Discuss Work-Related Pain

What the physiotherapist may assess

Workstation height, screen position and seating.

Time spent in one position and frequency of movement breaks.

Neck, shoulder, back and wrist movement.

Strength and endurance for sustained work.

Tasks outside work that may also influence symptoms.

What care may include

Practical workstation changes, movement breaks, symptom-management strategies, mobility and strengthening. The plan should be realistic for the patient's working day rather than a long routine that is unlikely to be followed.

FAQs

Is poor posture the only reason for neck pain?

No. Symptoms can be influenced by many factors. Posture and workstation setup are assessed in context rather than treated as the sole explanation.

Do I need an expensive ergonomic chair?

Not necessarily. Useful improvements can often be made through positioning, support and movement habits before purchasing new equipment.

Can this be done during a home visit?

Yes. A home visit is particularly useful when the workstation itself needs to be reviewed.
